Is marijuana really a 'gateway drug'?

For decades, anti-drug advocates have villified the green leaf for being a 'gateway' towards harder drugs, claiming that legalization would only lead to more drug overdoses. Now that a significant number of states have passed laws legalizing the use of marijuana for medical and recreational purposes, we can finally put this claim to the test.
